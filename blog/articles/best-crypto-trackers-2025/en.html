üí° Pro Tips for Using Crypto Trackers

1. Use Multiple Tools

Don't rely on just one tracker. Use Stock Crypto Alarm & Watch for instant alerts, CoinMarketCap for research, and TradingView for charting. Each tool has its strengths.

2. Set Up Price Alerts Strategically

Don't just track current prices. Set alerts at key levels:

  • Support and resistance zones
  • Your buy/sell targets
  • Stop-loss levels
  • All-time highs for breakout alerts

3. Track Multiple Exchanges

Crypto prices can vary significantly between exchanges. Arbitrage opportunities exist, but make sure to account for fees and transfer times.

4. Enable Browser Notifications

For time-sensitive alerts, browser notifications are faster than email or mobile app notifications. Make sure they're enabled!

5. Review Your Watchlist Regularly

Markets change. Update your watchlist monthly to remove underperformers and add promising new projects.

üéì Common Mistakes to Avoid
